## What is this?

The No-Show Cost Calculator is a free, open-source tool designed for service-based businesses that rely on appointment scheduling.

It helps you calculate:

- Monthly revenue lost due to no-shows
- Annual revenue impact
- Revenue recovered through rescheduling
- Potential gain if you reduce your no-show rate

---

- Clinics & healthcare providers
- Salons & aesthetic centers
- Consultants & coaches
- Independent professionals
- Any appointment-based business

If your income depends on booked time slots, this tool is for you.

## Why No-Shows Matter

Missed appointments are not just an inconvenience — they directly impact revenue, team productivity, and resource optimization.

Reducing no-shows by even 5–7% can significantly increase monthly income.

This calculator helps you visualize that impact instantly.

## Installation

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open `index.html` in your browser.
3. Start calculating immediately.

No build process required.

---

## Deploy on GitHub Pages

1. Go to **Settings → Pages**
2. Select **Deploy from branch**
3. Choose `main` and `/root`
4. Save

Your live calculator will be available at:

https://YOUR-ORG.github.io/timilyx-no-show-cost-calculator/
